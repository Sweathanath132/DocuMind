Sagility Company Facts (sample document for DocuMind demo)

Sagility is the largest pure-play healthcare Business Process Management (BPM) provider,
with over 24 years of domain expertise. Its corporate office is at AMR Tech Park,
Hongasandra, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560068.

Scale: Sagility reported US$658.3 million in revenue in FY25, growing 17.2% year over year.
The company has more than 40,000 employees across five countries: India, the Philippines,
the United States, Jamaica, and Colombia, operating in 17 cities. It processes more than
105 million claims annually and handles over 75 million customer interactions globally.

In November 2024, Sagility India Limited was listed on the National Stock Exchange of India
(NSE) and the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).

Five core healthcare practices: Claims, Engagement Services, Payment Integrity,
Clinical UM/CM, and Provider Network Operations. Proprietary platforms include BirchAI
(Gen AI-driven communication) and Provider Forward (provider data management).

Acquisitions: Devlin Consulting (analytics-led Payment Integrity), BirchAI (Gen AI),
and BroadPath (telehealth and remote services).

Innovation: The Document Processing Engine accelerates intake and classification of
healthcare documents using domain-trained OCR and NLP. Nurse Assist is an AI-powered
clinical review tool. Contract Central identifies claims overpayments.

Values: Sagility follows the S.O.A.R. values — Spark Curiosity, One Team One Direction,
Action for Results, and Right by Right Purpose.
